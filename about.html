NBA Twitter Analysis

Have you ever wanted to know how popular your favorite NBA team truly is? Did you ever think a lot of bandwagon fans just hopped on your favorite team. Well now there is a way to tell! We have collected data from 2012 till 2017 that tells us which team is the most tweeted about team in the world. Were the Warriors always this popular? How many people were talking about the Heat when LeBron won his first Championship? This are all questions that can be answered by our project. We have developed a a simple line graph that lets you pick the teams you want to compare and then displays how many tweets your team go.
